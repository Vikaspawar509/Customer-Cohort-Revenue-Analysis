# Insights from E-Commerce Data Analysis

## Revenue Insights
- Total revenue shows variation across months, indicating seasonal demand patterns  
- A small percentage of customers contributes a large share of total revenue  

## Customer Behavior
- A significant portion of customers make only one purchase  
- Repeat customers contribute more to long-term revenue  

## Retention Insights
- Customer retention drops significantly after the first month  
- Low retention indicates potential improvement areas in customer engagement  

## Cohort Analysis Insights
- Earlier cohorts show better retention compared to newer cohorts  
- Customer activity declines over time across all cohorts  

## Business Implications
- Focus on retaining high-value customers  
- Improve onboarding experience to reduce early churn  
- Target repeat customers with personalized strategies
